Tax & VAT Configuration

VAT Rate (VDS)

% Added to bill amount
VDS = Bill Amount × VAT%
e.g. 10,000 × 10% = 1,000

Tax Rate (TDS)

% Deducted at source
TDS = Bill Amount ÷ (1 + TAX%) × TAX%
e.g. 10,000 ÷ 1.03 × 3% = 291.26
